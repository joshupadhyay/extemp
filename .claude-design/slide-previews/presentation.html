The Problem

Impromptu speaking
is a trainable skill
with no good training tool.

  • 1 Students practice alone with no structured feedback on what went wrong or how to improve.
  • 2 Coaches can't give real-time, per-speech analysis at scale. Feedback is delayed and generic.
  • 3 Existing tools grade fluency but ignore the frameworks and structures that make speakers great.
The Solution

Extemp

AI coaching that understands structure, not just fluency. Practice impromptu speaking and get framework-aware feedback in seconds.

Framework Detection

Identifies your organizational structure and scores how well you executed it.

Instant Feedback

AI coaching in under 20 seconds. Specific, actionable, tied to your words.

Real Prompts

Topics drawn from actual extemp rounds across domestic and international affairs.

Progress Tracking

Every speech scored across dimensions. See patterns over time, not isolated grades.
